About

A bit about me.

I'm Drew Jones, an engineer based in Reno, Nevada. I build software, data pipelines, and ML systems. My current work focuses on LLM-enabled workflows, NLP, and production automation.

Before moving into software and data, I was an electromagnetic geophysicist. I have an M.Sc. in Geophysics from Memorial University of Newfoundland and a B.Sc. from the University of Nevada, Reno. I spent years doing field work and research in mining, geothermal, and oil & gas exploration before transitioning into data and ML engineering.

Outside of work I'm interested in optics, solar physics, archaeology, and building things.
