Edward Curtis Paperbacks

A 2+ year effort to digitize and publish all 20 volumes of The North American Indian in paperback for the first time.

Live site • Repo

Summary

This project started as a practical problem: the original volumes are difficult to access and expensive to collect. I wanted a clean, readable, affordable paperback set—so I digitized and rebuilt each volume, then published the full set.

  • Scope: 20 volumes
  • Timeline: ~2+ years
  • Outcome: Complete paperback set + simple storefront linking to Amazon

What I did

  • Digitized source material and rebuilt clean, print-ready interiors
  • Standardized layout, typography, and image handling across all volumes
  • Produced covers and metadata, and published each volume
  • Built a minimal website to present the set and route traffic to Amazon listings

Notes

The public site is intentionally simple: one page, fast loading, and focused on the books. Most links route directly to the Amazon product pages.
